# Implicit Functions

Implicit functions are typically invoked through syntactic sugar of various kinds.

 * `__assign` (`[]`)
 * `__append` (`[]`)
 * `__slice` (`identifier[]`)
 * `__plus` (`+`)
 * `__minus` (`-`)
 * `__add` (`+`)
 * `__subtract` (`-`)
 * `__multiply` (`*`)
 * `__divide` (`/`)
 * `__remainder` (`%`)
 * `__or` (`||`)
 * `__and` (`&&`)
 * `__not` (`!`)
